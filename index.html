About Me

In 2015, I moved to Los Angeles, California and have been pursuing my passions ever since! I love to travel and find new adventures. Travelling around the United States has given me the chance to explore my other passion, photography. When I'm not working on perfecting my coding skills, you can find me out and about taking photos and editing my work.
